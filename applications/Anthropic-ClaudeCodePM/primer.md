# Primer: Anthropic - Product Manager, Claude Code

## Role Context
- **Company Segment:** Frontier AI Model Developer
- **Product:** Claude Code (Agentic coding tool / CLI specifically for developers)
- **Key Requirement:** Combined 5+ years PM and engineering including 1 year as a professional engineer.

## Candidate Positioning & Strengths
- **The "Unlock":** 7 years of hands-on SWE experience at Google (Nest, Maps) is a rare asset for PM candidates. It provides instant credibility for a developer tools role. You know how engineers build software.
- **Agentic AI Depth:** You aren't just talking about agents; you've built the evaluation systems to measure their efficacy (Smartsheet/Brandfolder). Your understanding of the "50-metrics trap" and data fidelity issues is deep practitioner knowledge.
- **Rapid Prototyping:** Your instinct to "show, don't tell" (e.g., the Lovable semantic search prototype) proves you can move fast and test ideas with code, aligning with startup agility.

## Key Stories to Deploy (from Storybank)
1. **S001 - LLM-as-a-Judge Eval Harness:** Deploy for questions about AI technical depth, platform PM, and measuring non-deterministic systems.
   - *Opening Line:* "At Smartsheet I built the evaluation infrastructure that gave leadership the confidence to ship AI features—a 3-tiered system covering 100+ scenarios that became the standard for when agentic features were safe to launch."
   - *Earned Secret:* Non-deterministic AI needs a fundamentally different definition of "done." The failure isn't always the AI; sometimes it's the test.
2. **S007 - Semantic Search Prototype:** Deploy for questions about rapid experimentation, hard decisions, and pivoting a roadmap.

3. **Works with Nest Platform (Codegen):** Deploy for questions about developer platforms, API design, and scaling 0-to-1 solutions.
   - *Detail:* Built a protobuf + Jinja2 codegen system that auto-generated cross-platform interfaces, turning a weeks-long integration process into hours.

## Identified Gaps & Mitigations
- **Gap:** Most recent experience is enterprise B2B SaaS (Smartsheet/Brandfolder) rather than pure developer tooling.
  - *Mitigation:* Lean hard on the 7 years of SWE experience at Google and your daily use of AI coding tools (Claude Code, Cursor, this very Artemis skill). You are the target user.
- **Gap:** OpenHands rejection noted a lack of "rapid experimentation."
  - *Mitigation:* Over-index on the S007 Lovable prototype story. Make sure the opening line hits the "rapid experimentation" keyword explicitly.

## Interview Strategy & Reminders
- **Structure Over Rambling:** Open with the point (insight or result), not the context. One story per question. Anchor hard, don't enumerate frameworks.
- **The Data Fidelity Insight:** Don't bury your best insight. When discussing the eval harness, lead with the fact that data rots on live platforms and synthetic fixtures are brittle.
- **Authentic Voice:** You are a "builder and a tinkerer." Lean into it. You build things that enrich people's lives.
