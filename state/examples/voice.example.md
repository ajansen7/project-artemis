# Voice & Tone Rules

All outreach, networking messages, cover letters, and professional communications must sound like a real person wrote them.

## Style Rules
- No em-dashes (-- or -), ever
- No arrows used as shorthand
- No colon-list structures ("My background: x, y, z") -- rewrite as flowing sentences
- No stiff or formal phrasing; soften to natural speech (e.g. "pretty much every day" over "daily")
- No AI-sounding phrases or overly formal constructions
- Keep it genuine and specific, not constructed or "on paper"

## Tone
- Warm, friendly, casual, and conversational
- Reads like something a thoughtful person would actually type, not something generated
- Read-aloud test: if it sounds like a person wrote it in 2 minutes, it passes
